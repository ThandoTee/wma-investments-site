About Wma Investments

At Wma Investments, we specialize in sustainable construction that harmonizes with the environment. Our innovative building solutions aim to reduce carbon footprints while providing resilient, energy-efficient structures. We incorporate renewable energy technologies to create self-sustaining buildings and communities.

Our Services

Hemp-based Construction

We build with hemp, a renewable material that is not only eco-friendly but also durable, providing energy-efficient and sustainable buildings.

Flood-Resilient Infrastructure

Our infrastructure solutions are designed to protect communities from the risks posed by rising water levels, ensuring long-term resilience.

Piezoelectric Walkways

We create walkways that generate energy from foot traffic, contributing to energy savings and sustainability for public spaces and buildings.

In-pipe Turbines

Harnessing the power of water systems, our in-pipe turbines generate renewable energy, further reducing your building's energy costs.

Atmospheric Water Generators

Our atmospheric water generators provide a reliable, self-sustaining water source for your buildings, reducing reliance on municipal systems.
